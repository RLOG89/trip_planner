Welcome!

Welcome to the all new ScotTrip Planner, which has been designed to make it easier for you to plan your Scotland trip, get cost estimates and work out your travel mileage.

To improve how you find out about activities along your route we have developed a separate section which gives you the ability to create and plan a trip and add them and save them to an itinerary.

The route finder service provides maps and driving directions for the Scotland, as well as giving you the opportunity to find distilleries, movie locations, Historic sites and sporting venues.

ScotTrip is always looking for new ways to help you plan your journey.

A massive thanks from the ScotTrip team for visiting and please spread the word.
